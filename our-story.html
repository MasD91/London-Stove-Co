Our Story

The London Stove Company was set up by Ross Spurrier in 2015, starting out as a heating engineer the business expanded and gradually became popular. We are a reliable, trustworthy and professional company who pride ourselves on outstanding customer satisfaction. We enjoy what we do and this comes across in our workmanship and attention to detail.

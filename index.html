ABOUT US

Company Values and Customer Focus

The company prides itself on being reliable, trustworthy, and professional. They prioritize customer satisfaction, taking great care in supplying and fitting stoves. They highlight their outstanding workmanship, attention to detail, and the importance of glowing references.

Expert Solutions for Chimneyless Spaces

In situations without a conventional chimney, the company offers a twinwall stainless steel insulated flue system. This system is highly versatile, able to navigate internally through ceilings, floors, attics, or vertically through walls to resolve chimney absence issues.

Benefits of Chimney Lining

Chimney lining facilitates a direct link between the woodburner and outside air, reducing soot and tar buildup within the liner. This improves stove performance by allowing flue gases to warm up faster.

Environmental Friendliness of Wood Stoves

Wood, as a renewable fuel, is considered carbon neutral. The company emphasizes that burning wood releases the same amount of carbon dioxide it absorbed while growing. Wood stoves provide direct heat to homes, reducing reliance on central heating systems.
